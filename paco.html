Paco Diehl-Citoli

July 17, 2007 - February 14, 2019

Paco came to us the fall of 2009. His backyard size and confidence increased over the years. I am proud to say that other than a few days he spent the last 4 1/2 years roaming these 6 acres with complete freedom. I just saw him head up the hill in his last week. I witnessed Paco go from being the first dog at the dog park to get humped by another dog to a fierce and brave best friend. He spent his last ounce of energy wagging his tail.
